# Social Media Pre-Launch Strategy

## Overview
Pre-launch educational brand awareness campaign across 3 channels. No UGC available - using stock visuals (Australian birding scene). Content created in Canva.

## Channel Strategy

| Channel | Audience | Focus |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **Facebook** | Birders 45-70 | Trust, education, species content |
| **LinkedIn** | Tour operators (B2B) | Operator pain points, business value |
| **Instagram** | Younger birders 25-45 | Visual, aspirational, community |

## First Posts

### LinkedIn
- **Format:** 1200x627px image + long text
- **Message:** "67% of small group tours struggle to meet minimums" - addresses operator pain
- **CTA:** "If you've ever cancelled a tour... I'd like to hear your story"
- **Visual:** Stat graphic on Deep Eucalyptus background

### Facebook  
- **Format:** 1200x1200px square
- **Message:** "847 Australian species. Which ones are you still chasing?"
- **CTA:** "Drop your #1 chase bird in comments"
- **Visual:** Bird photo (Superb Fairy-wren) with gradient overlay

### Instagram
- **Format:** 5-slide carousel, 1080x1080px each
- **Flow:** Hook → Problem → Solution → CTA
- **Message:** "Your chase list is about to get easier"
- **CTA:** "Drop your #1 chase bird in the comments"

## Content Pillars

**Facebook (4-5/week):**
- Australian Species Stories (40%)
- Quorum Mechanics Explained (25%)
- Operator Spotlights (20%)
- Community Engagement (15%)

**LinkedIn (2-3/week):**
- Quorum Advantage for Operators (35%)
- Trust in Adventure Economy (25%)
- Niche Tourism Thought Leadership (25%)
- Success Stories (15%)

**Instagram (3-4 feed + daily stories + 2-3 reels/week):**
- Australian Birding Beauty (40%)
- Species Stories Carousels (25%)
- Birder Community/Culture (20%)
- Tour Opportunities (15%)

## 8-Week Timeline
- Weeks 1-2: Facebook authority building
- Weeks 3-4: Instagram ramp-up
- Weeks 5-6: Community launch + B2B LinkedIn growth
- Weeks 7-8: Pre-launch intensity
- Week 9: Public launch

## Brand Voice
- No hype, all substance
- Trust-focused
- No fake urgency or scarcity
- Educational over promotional
- Proof-based claims
