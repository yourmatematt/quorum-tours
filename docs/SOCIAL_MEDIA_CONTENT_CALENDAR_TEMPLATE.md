### A/B Testing (Weeks 3-8)

- **Post timing:** Test 9 AM vs. 12 PM vs. 3 PM (track engagement)
- **Hashtag count:** Test 10 vs. 20 hashtags (track reach)
- **Reel length:** Test 15-sec vs. 30-sec vs. 45-sec (track completion rate)
- **Caption length:** Test short vs. long captions (track comment CTR)
- **Carousel slides:** Test 5-slide vs. 7-slide carousels (track swipes/engagement)

### Monthly Review (End of Months 1-3)

- Compare metrics to targets
- Identify top-performing content types
- Adjust content mix for next month
- Gather qualitative feedback from community
- Refine messaging based on engagement patterns
