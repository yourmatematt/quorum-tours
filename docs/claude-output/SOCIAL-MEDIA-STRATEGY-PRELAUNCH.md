# Quorum Tours: Pre-Launch Social Media Strategy
## Comprehensive Channel Strategy (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram)

**Created:** January 27, 2026
**Phase:** Pre-Launch Brand Awareness & Trust Building
**Duration:** 8 weeks pre-launch, 4 weeks soft launch
**Channels:** Facebook | LinkedIn | Instagram
**Audience:** Birders (45-70), Tour Operators, Younger Birders (25-45)

---

## STRATEGIC FOUNDATION

### Core Value Propositions (Universal)
1. **Risk-Free Discovery** – No payment until your tour confirms
2. **Quorum Guarantees** – Tours only confirm when enough birders commit (real bookings)
3. **Species-First Search** – Find tours by the birds you want to see
4. **Trust Economics** – Deposit waived after first tour, reviews from real birders
5. **Operator Empowerment** – No more cancelled tours, predictable income, real demand signals

### Brand Voice Pillars
- **Authentic** – Real birder perspectives, not marketing speak
- **Educational** – Teach about birding, Australian species, platform mechanics
- **Trust-Focused** – Address skepticism directly, transparency first
- **Conversational** – Talk WITH the community, not AT them
- **Proof-Based** – Data, mechanics, and real examples beat promises

### Constraints & Resources
- **Status:** Pre-Launch (no product to sell yet, purely educational/brand awareness)
- **Visuals:** Stock only (Australian birding scenes via Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay)
- **Design:** All content created in Canva
- **Geography:** Australian birding scene focus
- **No UGC:** No original photos or videos

## FACEBOOK STRATEGY

**Primary Audience:** Serious, experienced birders (45-70), retirees, weekend naturalists

### Audience Profile
- **Behavior:** Active in local birding groups, skeptical of digital solutions, value peer reviews
- **Pain Points:** Hard to find tours matching target species, unreliable group sizes, wasted money on cancelled tours
- **Strengths:** Understand value, loyal to trusted sources, word-of-mouth influence
- **Median Income:** Professional/middle-class, willing to spend on quality experiences

### Content Pillars (4)

#### Pillar 1: "Australian Species Stories" (30% of content)
Educational deep-dives into Australian birds, season-specific guides, migration patterns, real birder accounts.
- *Purpose:* Build authority, provide value, create save-worthiness
- *Frequency:* 5-7 posts per week
- *CTA:* "Find tours targeting THIS bird on Quorum Tours"

#### Pillar 2: "Quorum Mechanics Explained" (25%)
How the system works without jargon, why it solves real problems, operator benefits, transparency.
- *Purpose:* Address skepticism, build trust in platform
- *Frequency:* 2-3 posts per week
- *CTA:* "How would your birding change if you could say yes without losing money?"

#### Pillar 3: "Operator Spotlights" (20%)
Feature established tour operators, their story, regions, specialties, how they benefit from guaranteed minimums.
- *Purpose:* Build trust through association, humanize platform
- *Frequency:* 1-2 posts per week
- *CTA:* "Meet the guides you'll trust with your birding list"

#### Pillar 4: "Birding Community Engagement" (15%)
Ask questions about target species, polls, celebrate communities, conservation stories, monthly discussions.
- *Purpose:* Build community, drive engagement, gather insights
- *Frequency:* 1-2 posts per week
- *CTA:* Direct questions and polls

### Posting Schedule
- **Frequency:** 4-5 posts per week
- **Best Times:**
  - Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM (morning coffee checking)
  - Wednesday, 6-7 PM (evening browsing)
- **Content Mix:** 40% educational | 25% mechanics | 20% spotlights | 15% engagement

### Engagement Strategy

**Page Approach:**
- Create Quorum Tours brand page
- Post 3x weekly educational content
- Weekly "Ask the Community" questions
- Respond to ALL comments within 4 hours
- Pin educational content about risk-free model

**Groups Approach (CRITICAL):**

## ENGAGEMENT & RESPONSE PROTOCOLS

- **Facebook:** 500-800 followers, 5-8% engagement rate, 20+ daily page visits
- **LinkedIn:** 200-400 followers, 8-12% engagement rate, 10+ operator inquiries
- **Instagram:** 1,000-1,500 followers, 6-10% engagement rate, 15+ daily profile visits
- **Email signups:** 150-250 from social traffic (conversion tracking)
- **Operator inquiries:** 20-40 via LinkedIn messaging

### Qualitative Success Indicators
- Positive sentiment across all channels (no significant negativity)
- Meaningful comments and discussions (not vanity likes)
- Operator and community authority established
- Clear understanding of value proposition (user feedback validation)
- No objections to platform mechanics or pricing
- Community asks for launch date

### Content Performance Benchmarks
- **Species education posts:** 12-15% engagement (high-value, saveable)
- **Operator spotlights:** 10-12% engagement (trust-building)
- **Community questions:** 8-10% engagement (participation)
- **Product/feature teasers:** 4-6% engagement (awareness, save for later)
- **CTAs and conversions:** 2-4% click-through rate

## COMPETITIVE CONTEXT

### Why This Strategy Wins

**vs. Generic Travel/Tourism Content:**
- Species-specific positioning (niche beats broad reach)
- Trust focus beats typical marketing hype
- Educational content has longer lifespan and shareability
- Community engagement beats one-way broadcasting

**vs. Individual Tour Operators:**
- Platform positioning beats individual operator branding
- Operator empowerment messaging (partnership, not competition)
- Species search and quorum mechanics are genuinely novel
- Aggregated reviews and trust system defensible

**vs. Other Booking Platforms:**
- Depth beats breadth (birding expertise, not generalist)
- Operator-first approach (mutual respect, not extraction)
- Transparent mechanics (no hidden fees or pressure tactics)
- Community as moat (network effects, not algorithm dependence)

## FINAL NOTES

### What This Strategy Avoids
- **No fake urgency or scarcity** (trust over pressure)
- **No hype or marketing speak** (authenticity over persuasion)
- **No generic birding content** (Quorum-specific value always)
- **No aggressive selling pre-launch** (education and awareness only)
- **No unverified claims** (transparency and mechanics-based messaging)

### What This Strategy Emphasizes
- **Risk-free mechanics** (real differentiator vs. competitors)
- **Operator empowerment** (genuine partnership narrative)
- **Species-first positioning** (niche advantage)
- **Community building** (long-term moat)
- **Educational value** (trust through knowledge)

### Flexibility Points
- Seasonal content can shift based on bird migration calendars
- Operator spotlights can expand or contract based on availability
- Reel frequency can increase if they outperform static posts
- Story frequency can adjust based on team capacity
- Hashtag mix can evolve based on platform algorithm changes

This strategy is designed to be executed, measured, and optimized continuously. The 8-week pre-launch phase is an opportunity to establish brand authority, build community trust, and create demand for the launch period.
