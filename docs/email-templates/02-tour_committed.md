# Tour Committed Email

## Metadata
- **Template ID:** tour_committed
- **Trigger:** User commits to a forming tour
- **Audience:** Birders who just committed
- **Key Goal:** Confirm commitment, manage expectations, explain next steps

---

## Subject Line
Your commitment to [TOUR_NAME] is confirmed

## Preview Text
Now we wait to see if the tour reaches quorum by [DEADLINE_DATE].

---

## Email Body

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Your commitment to **[TOUR_NAME]** on [TOUR_DATE] with [OPERATOR_NAME] is confirmed.

**What this means:**
- You are committed to this tour if it reaches quorum by [DEADLINE_DATE].
- Deposit paid: [DEPOSIT_AMOUNT] (applied to your total when you pay the balance)
- If no deposit was required, you'll pay the full amount when the tour confirms.

**Current status:**
- Commitments needed: [QUORUM_NUMBER]
- Current commitments: [CURRENT_COMMITS]
- Spots remaining: [SPOTS_REMAINING]

**What happens next:**

If the tour reaches [QUORUM_NUMBER] committed participants by [DEADLINE_DATE], you'll receive an email saying the tour is confirmed. You'll then have 24 hours to pay the balance of [REMAINING_AMOUNT]. Your deposit is applied to the total.

If the tour doesn't reach quorum, your deposit is refunded in full. No penalties. We'll let you know the tour isn't running.

No action needed. You can monitor the tour's progress on your account dashboard.
