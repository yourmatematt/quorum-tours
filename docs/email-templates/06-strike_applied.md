# Strike Applied Email

## Metadata
- **Template ID:** strike_applied
- **Trigger:** User misses payment deadline
- **Audience:** Users with a missed payment
- **Key Goal:** Inform clearly about strike, explain impact, outline appeal process

---

## Subject Line
Your account has received a strike

## Preview Text
You missed the payment deadline for [TOUR_NAME]. Here's what happens next.

---

## Email Body

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

You did not pay for [TOUR_NAME] within the 24-hour deadline. Your deposit has been forfeited to the operator.

**What just happened:**

Your deposit: [DEPOSIT_AMOUNT] (minus 3% platform fee = [DEPOSIT_LESS_FEE]) has been sent to [OPERATOR_NAME].

Your spot went to the next person on the waitlist.

You've received a **Strike 1** on your account.

---

**What this means for future bookings:**

Strike 1: Operators can require a deposit on your next tour commitment. The operator sets the amount.

Strike 2: You'll be required to pay a 50% deposit on all future tours.

Strike 3: Your account will be suspended. You won't be able to book tours.

---

**Can you appeal?**

Yes, but only if there were extenuating circumstances. Reply to this email or contact us at [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with:
- What prevented you from paying.
- Any supporting information (hospital visit, family emergency, etc.).
- A request to review your case.

We'll review your appeal and respond within 3 business days. Approved appeals may result in the strike being removed.

**Questions?**

Contact us at [SUPPORT_EMAIL].

Best,
The Quorum Team
